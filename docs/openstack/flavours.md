Flavours
========

The following virtual machine flavours are supported on Ilifu:

| Name    | VCPUS | RAM (GB) | Disk (GB) |
|---------|-------|----------|-----------|
| ilifu-A | 1     | 4        | 20        |
| ilifu-B | 2     | 8        | 20        |
| ilifu-C | 2     | 8        | 20        |
| ilifu-D | 4     | 32       | 20        |
| ilifu-E | 8     | 64       | 20        |
| liifu-F | 16    | 124      | 20        |
| ilifu-G | 32    | 250      | 20        |
| ilifu-H | 32    | 500      | 20        |
